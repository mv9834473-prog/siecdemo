About SIEC Education (Best Study Abroad Consultants)

SIEC is a premier Overseas Education, Migration Services, and Recruitment Consulting Agency, a Gold member of the British Council of India, and certified by AAERI, AIRC, and ICEF. SIEC is the best study abroad consultant in Delhi, with a highly trusted team of consultants in India for studying abroad. We are the top overseas education consultants in Delhi with a 99% success rate. Established in 1995, our vast branch network spans across 3 continents, including 28 offices across India and 11 overseas branches in Canada, Australia, Pakistan, Nepal, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka and Bhutan.
